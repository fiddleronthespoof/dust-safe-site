## The problem
Chasing walls indoors fills rooms with fine dust fast. In occupied buildings, spread matters as much as exposure.

**Printable checklist for this type of work (PDF):**  
👉 [Indoor Dust Control – 5 Quick Checklists (UK)](/downloads/indoor-dust-control-5-checklists-uk.pdf)

## What works in practice
- Prefer **on-tool extraction** with a proper shroud.
- Use **wet methods** where the finish allows.
- Keep others out of the room and contain spread.

## Minimum viable setup
- Wall chaser with shroud + compatible extractor  
- FFP3 disposable or half mask + P3 filters  
- No dry sweeping during or after the job
